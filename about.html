About MXP Public Foundation

Learn about our journey, our people, and our commitment to creating lasting positive change in communities worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, MXP Public Foundation began as a small community initiative with a big vision: to create sustainable change that empowers communities and transforms lives. What started as a local effort has grown into a global movement.

Our founders recognized that lasting change comes from working with communities, not for them. This philosophy continues to guide everything we do today.

Message from the Founder

Shri Jaiki Kumar

"It is with immense pride and gratitude that I reflect on the journey of MXP Public Foundation since its inception in 2015. What began as a simple desire to give back to the community has blossomed into a powerful force for positive change. Our success is a testament to the dedication of our team, the unwavering support of our volunteers and donors, and the resilience of the communities we serve. We are committed to transparency, sustainability, and empowering those in need to build brighter futures."

Trust Deed Summary

The Trust Deed of MXP Public Foundation outlines our legal framework, governance structure, and commitment to our stated mission. It ensures transparency and accountability in all our operations. Key aspects include the establishment of the board of trustees, guidelines for fund utilization, and our dedication to charitable activities focused on education, housing, healthcare, and community development. A full copy is available for public review to ensure complete transparency with our stakeholders.
